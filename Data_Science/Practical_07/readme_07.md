# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🧪 Experiment No: 07

## 📌 Title: Sales Performance Reporting using Power BI

---

## 📖 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing the sales performance of a retail company using Power BI.
The dashboard provides key insights into sales trends, product performance, and regional distribution.

---

## 📂 Dataset

* **Source:** Kaggle
* **Dataset:** Retail Sales Dataset
* **Path:** `Dataset/retail_sales_dataset.csv`
* **Link:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mohammadtalib786/retail-sales-dataset

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Identify top-performing products
* Understand regional sales distribution
* Track monthly sales trends

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* 💰 Total Sales (KPI Card)
* 📦 Total Units Sold
* 🏆 Top 5 Products by Sales
* 🌍 Sales by Region (Map Visualization)
* 📈 Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)

---

## 🎛️ Filters Used

* Product Category
* Region
* Time Period

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Power Query (Data Cleaning)
* DAX (Calculated Measures)

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Identified top-performing products contributing to maximum revenue
* Observed seasonal trends in monthly sales
* Compared sales distribution across different regions
